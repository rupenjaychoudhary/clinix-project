OUR DOCTORS

Dr. Anjana Satyajit

Tooth Specialist

Dr. Anjana is a dedicated dentist with an experience of 23 years. She has a team of specialists that include Endodontists, Orthodontist, Periodontist, Pedodontist, Prosthdontist, Oral Surgeon and Implantologist who work meticulously to offer the best treatments. She has published several papers both nationally and internationally and is a member of many medical associations.

Dr. Gobu

Heart Specialist

Dr. Gobu is an eminent Cardiologist at Gleneagles Global Health City, Chennai. He has more than two decades of experience in the field of Interventional Cardiology. Dr. Gobu’s medical interests are Radial Approach Angiography, Non-Invasive Cardiology, Patent Foramen Ovale, Peripheral Angioplasty, Acute Aortic Dissection, etc. He is an active member of the Cardiological Society of India (CSI) and Tamil Nadu Medical Council. Dr. Gobu has more than 21+ years of experience in his field.

Dr. P Vijay Anand Reddy

Eye Specialist

Dr. P Vijay Anand Reddy is a leading and a highly qualified Ophthalomologist with a practice of +28 years and specialised focus on ocular oncology. Dr. Reddy is a professional member of the, American Society of Clinical Oncology (ASCO), European Society of Medical Oncology (ESMO), American Society for Therapeutic Oncology (ASTRO), Association of Radiation Oncologists of India (AROI) and the Indian Society of Oncology (ISO).

Dr. Amin Kaba

Child Specialist

Dr. Amin Kaba has completed MBBS from the University of Mumbai, Mumbai in 1996, a Diploma in Child Health (DCH) from College of Physicians and Surgeons Mumbai in 2001, and MD - Pediatrics from University of Mumbai, Mumbai in 2002. He has experience of 2 decades in the field of Paediatrics. He is recognized as a Teacher for Pediatrics for the College of Physicians and Surgeons. He has been a reviewer for Pediatrics-related articles for IJRI (Indian Journal of Radiology and Imaging) and also a reviewer for Pediatriconcall (online journal). .
